Welcome to the official public assets repository for the East West University Telecommunications Club (EWUTC).

Founded on 14 April 2006, EWUTC is one of the most active student-run organizations at East West University. The club works to promote technological excellence, professional growth, innovation, and knowledge sharing among students.

## Our Mission

EWUTC is dedicated to:

- promoting knowledge and awareness of modern telecommunications and information technology
- encouraging innovation, entrepreneurship, and research-driven learning
- providing practical exposure to emerging technologies and industry practices
- supporting students in developing technical, professional, and leadership skills
- creating opportunities for collaboration between academia and industry
